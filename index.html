Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any business experience to join?
Not at all! This expedition is designed for complete beginners. We start from the very basics and build up step by step. If you have a business idea (or even just the desire to start something), you're ready to begin.
How much time do I need each day?
Plan for 20-30 minutes total per day: 5-7 minutes for the video lesson, 10-15 minutes to complete the worksheet, and 10-15 minutes for the daily summit challenge. Everything is designed to fit into a busy schedule.
What if I miss a day or fall behind?
No problem! All materials remain available throughout the entire expedition and beyond. You can catch up at your own pace. The most important thing is to keep moving forward, even if you're not on the exact daily schedule.
Is this really free? What's the catch?
Yes, completely free! This is our gift to aspiring entrepreneurs in the Big Bear community. The only "catch" is that expedition graduates receive a free 4-week trial to the Big Bear Chamber of Commerce, which we hope you'll find valuable enough to continue.
What kind of business can I start with this program?
Any type! The principles we teach work for service businesses, product companies, online ventures, brick-and-mortar stores, and everything in between. The focus is on building strong fundamentals that apply to any business model.
Who will be leading this expedition?
This expedition is led by experienced local business owners and Chamber members who have successfully built and scaled their own ventures in the Big Bear community. You'll get real-world guidance from people who understand the local business landscape.
What happens after I complete the expedition?
You'll receive an official Summit Certificate and a 4-week free trial to the Big Bear Chamber of Commerce. Plus, you'll have lifetime access to all expedition materials and be invited to exclusive quarterly "Summit Club" meetups for ongoing support.
Can I get support if I have questions during the expedition?
Absolutely! You can reply to any daily expedition email with questions, and our expedition guides personally respond within 24 hours. Think of us as your personal expedition support team committed to helping you reach the summit.
